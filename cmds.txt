/timetable - to get today's timetable
/timetable <day> - to get timetable of <day> specified below:
        mon - for Monday,
        tue - for Tuesday,
        wed - for Wednesday,
        thu - for Thursday,
        fri - for Friday,
        sat - for Saturday
    Example command: '/timetable mon' will give monday's timetable
/tomorrow - to get tomorrow timetable
/current - to get current live class
/next - to get next class
/start - to get started
/help - to get help related to bot
